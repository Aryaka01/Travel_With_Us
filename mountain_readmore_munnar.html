BOOK A STAY IN A TREE HOUSE

Staying in an eco-friendly treehouse made from natural materials such as choir, bamboo, straw and hay, and waking up to the chirping of birds is one of the best things to do in Munnar.


TREK TO CHEEYAPPARA WATERFALLS

The magnificent Cheeyappara Waterfalls, falling from a height of 1000ft above sea level, is one of the best things to see in Munnar. It is 7-step cascading waterfalls snuggled amidst dense forests and offers you trekking and hiking opportunities.


ELEPHANT SAFARI AT CARMELAGIRI ELEPHANT PARK

If you love safaris, then going on an elephant safari in the Carmelagiri Elephant Park is one of the top things to do in Munnar. The park is located on the Mattupetty road and offers tourists short rides of around 10-15 minutes on the well-tamed elephants.
Ticket: Around INR 400 per person for short safaris of 15 minutes.
Horse Riding: Cost around INR 300 per head.


HIKE TO ANAMUDI PEAK

It offers a bewildering hiking trail with stunning view of the scenic landscape, small brooklets and dense foliage. If you love travelling and exploring places on your pace, hiking to Anamudi Peak is one of the best things to do in Munnar to embrace nature and in its overwhelming charm.


VISIT THE BLOSSOM INTERNATIONAL PARK

It is a 16km long trail taking you through the dense and dark forest that forms a canopy so heavy that it blocks the sunrays even during the day time. It takes around 7-8 hours to complete your trek to the highest peak.

RELISH AUTHENTIC CUISINE

Food in Munnar is authentic Kerala cuisine that you can enjoy in any local of fine-dine restaurants here. One can relish vegetarian and non-vegetarian Kerala food cooked in the traditional way and served to you hot and fresh on banana leaves. Taking pleasure in having mouthfuls of lip-smacking dishes is also one of the top things to do in Munnar, and we highly recommend you try Idiyappam with egg curry, appam & stew, puttu with kadala curry or the wide variety of sea-food.


RELAX WITH AN AYURVEDIC SPA

Treating yourself to a rejuvenating and relaxing Ayurvedic spa is one of the best thing to do in Munnar. Kerala is famous for its Ayurvedic therapies and spas and Munnar is also a haven for the most amazing and refreshing spas. You can book Ayurvedic massage, wellness treatment, massage for mind and body relaxation or the authentic shirodhara.
