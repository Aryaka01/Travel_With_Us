EXPLORE THE SCENIC VELLAYANI LAKE

Famed for its picture-perfect landscapes witnessing this place is one of the best things to do in Kovalam. This largest freshwater source extends mind-blowing vistas and its realm multiples as the sun sets down. The sparkling water contrasting with the starlit sky is a frame which is a treat to the eyes. One visiting Kovalam must definitely add this place in their list of destination as it is a popular tourist spot.


EXPERIENCE SPIRITUAL VIBES AT SHRI PADMANABHASWAMY TEMPLE

Located in Thiruvananthapuram, Shri Padmanabhaswamy temple exhibits exceptionally beautiful architecture which is a blend of Tamil and authentic Kerala style. Featuring elegantly carved walls and mesmerizing aura of serenity, this place is indeed a holy abode. Here, you can spot the deity of Para Brahmana who is the supreme Brahman. The best time to witness this temple is in October as at this time the Alpashy festival is celebrated with great zeal and enthusiasm by the devotees, and the temple is wonderfully decorated.


TASTE THE LUXURY WITH HOUSEBOAT CRUISING

Explore the backwaters of Kerala in a houseboat! This is as amazing and exciting as it sounds. These houseboats cater to each and every facility, be it the air-conditioning or delicious delights you can have everything in here. The cruise begins with warm welcoming with fresh coconuts and beautiful Jasmine garlands. Later, you can embrace the allure of Kerala’s backwaters and you can also indulge in fishing, coir making, and various other activities while you enjoy the cruise. Spending a day one this unique experience is something you must not miss out, as it is one of the best things to do in Kovalam.


CAPTURE THE SPLENDID VIEW OF LIGHTHOUSE BEACH

Located in Kovalam (Southern Kerala), the Lighthouse Beach is known for the 35 metres tall Vizhinjam Lighthouse, located on the top of the Kurumkal hillock. The lighthouse is entirely made of stones and coloured in red and white bands. Visitors love to reach the top of this tall structure by using a spiral staircase to click some memorable photographs. Lighthouse beach is a perfect spot to enjoy water activities and sports, such as surfing, parasailing and paragliding.


EMBRACE THE BEAUTY OF KARAMANA RIVER

Embarking from Agastyar Kodama, Karamana River is a place which will never fail to amaze you with its enchanting realm. Serenity is interwoven in the aura of the region and is just perfect for spending quality time, embracing the scenic wonders. In the end, the long flowing river merges with the Arabian sea and the river basin exhibits a spectacular range of lush green plants. Witnessing this place is indeed one of the most impressive things to do in Kovalam.


DELVE IN THE PLACID AURA OF THE HAWA BEACH

Boasting with its tranquil settings, visiting this beach is one of the best things to do in Kovalam. Renowned as Eve’s beach, this place is one spot for scrumptious seafood, aqua adventures, luxury hotels and everything a vacationer needs. Here you can gaze at the stunning coastline of the beach and also you can a long boat ride while you witness the beach. One must not miss out on the sunset of this beach, as this view will refresh each and every segment of the body.


PUMP UP YOUR ADRENALINE WITH WATER ACTIVITIES

Is adventure your thing and you are looking to for something exciting? Enjoy your vacation to the fullest and try exciting aqua sports at the beaches of Kovalam. From white water surfing to rapid motorboat rides, here you can have every little thing. Imagine surfing on the high tides, feeling the fresh sea breeze tickle all over your face, giving you a tincture of thrill. Well, this can be endured at lighthouse beach. Not only this, here you can have a whole lot of freakish experiences which will make your tour even more exceptional. Also, here you can encounter windsurfing which is an activity worth trying. Make the best of your trip and witness the fun-filled adventures in Kovalam.


DISCOVER THE MARINEE LIFE AT VIZHINJAM MARINE AQUARIUM

Discover the Marine Life at Vizhinjam Marine Aquarium Does marine life bewitch you? Make a visit to the Vizhinjam Marine Aquarium which is a must-visit attraction of the coastal town. Aquatic life like Angelfish, clownfish, seahorses, boxfish, cowfish, eels and numerous others can be spotted here. Something which makes this place unique is the Image pearl technique, with which you can create a pearl of any random shape using the cement shell. Here, you can also find an unusual reef tank which has an attractive collection of variants of corals.


SAVOUR DELIGHT OF GERMAN BAKERY

Looking for a good place to eat? Hit out to the German bakery which serves lip-smacking delights and is very popular amongst the youngsters. From mouth-watering pancakes to exotic Thai food here you can find every little thing.
